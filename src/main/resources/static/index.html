About Kongunadu Arts and Science College

Established on 12 August 1973 and located in the industrial city of Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, Kongunadu Arts and Science College (Autonomous) has been a pioneer in higher education for over 50 years. Granted Autonomous status by the UGC in the academic year 2004–05, the college is re-accredited with an 'A+' Grade by NAAC (4th Cycle) with an outstanding CGPA of 3.64/4.00, and recognized as a College of Excellence by the UGC.

The institution maintains a strong focus on academic excellence, industrial research, women empowerment, and social justice, nurturing holistic growth and professional readiness in our students.
